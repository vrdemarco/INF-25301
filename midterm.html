Celebrate Michigan’s apple season at Hy’s Cider Mill. Bring your family and friends to enjoy crisp autumn air, fresh apples, and our famous cider and donuts.

Pick-Your-Own Schedule

  • Weekends only: September 7 – October 27
  • Hours: 11:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.
  • Varieties: Honeycrisp, Fuji, Gala, and more

Things to Do

There’s more than apples at Hy’s!

  • Enjoy fresh cider and donuts
  • Visit the pumpkin patch
  • Take a wagon ride through the orchard
  • Shop for local honey and jams

Plan Your Visit

We’re located in Romeo, Michigan — just 45 minutes north of Detroit. Admission is free; pay only for what you pick!
